1. Core thematic statement
2. Protagonist profile (deep psychology, flaw, desire, fear, contradiction)

4. 3-act structure
5. 12–20 chapter outline (each chapter with:
   - External conflict
   - Internal conflict
   - Escalation
   - Narrative purpose)
6. Character arc progression across the book

8. The emotional tone progression
